Experience

From the Kitchen to the Keyboard: A Chef's Journey

For over a decade, my world was the heat of the kitchen, the thrill of a busy service, and the joy of creating something exceptional from scratch. As a chef, I've led teams in fast-paced environments like the award-winning Adare Manor and developed new culinary concepts for the Bakestone Group.

My experience as a Head Chef and Development Chef taught me more than just how to cook; it taught me how to lead, how to innovate under pressure, and how to manage complex projects from concept to completion. The precision required to perfect a recipe is not so different from the logic needed to write clean code. The creativity of designing a new menu mirrors the innovation required to build a new application.

Now, I am channeling that same passion, discipline, and creative energy into a new craft: software development. I am excited to bring my unique perspective and a well-honed set of transferable skills to an entry-level position or apprenticeship where I can continue to learn and grow.
